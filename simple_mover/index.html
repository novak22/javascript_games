Touch controls

Quick controls

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move the blue square. Collect stars to gain points and avoid the red walls!

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move
  • On-screen pad: Tap arrows & Dash on touch devices
  • Space: Dash (short burst of speed)
  • R: Restart instantly after a crash

Colliding with a wall pauses the action briefly before you respawn in a now-larger arena—press R if you want to restart immediately. On touch screens the on-screen pad keeps you moving while the arena resets.
